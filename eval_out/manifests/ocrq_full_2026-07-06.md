# Run manifest — OCR-Quality full 1000 (comparative + adjudication)

*First manifest under the [evidence_plan](../../Docs/dev_notes/evidence_plan.md) durability
rule. Comparative agreement vs the Qwen2.5-VL-72B `ocr_text` pseudo-reference — NOT accuracy
(the reference's own quality is what `human_score` rates; trust agreement most on score-1).*

## Run

- **Code state:** the run predates the PR #21 hallucination guards (its failures motivated

## Scores (CORRECTED metric — CJK-aware tokenisation, PR #20)

The original `results.csv` was written with the pre-fix whitespace tokeniser (word metrics
~0 on Chinese regardless of quality). Re-scored 2026-07-06 from the saved `ours/*.txt`
transcripts with the fixed metric; 601/1000 word-metric values changed. The pre-fix CSV is
archived as `results.pre_cjk_fix.csv` (append-only; nothing destroyed).

Mean `agree_recall` (word-level agreement with the 72B reference), by language × the
reference's human quality score:

| lang | s1 (best ref) | s2 | s3 | s4 (worst ref) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| en | 0.945 (n=179) | 0.895 (n=90) | 0.760 (n=20) | 0.364 (n=21) |
| zh | 0.926 (n=317) | 0.875 (n=212) | 0.678 (n=64) | 0.340 (n=83) |

Aggregate: **score-1 agreement 0.934**; all pages 0.836. Agreement declines monotonically
with the *reference's* rated quality — the expected signature if the reference (not our
engine) is the main variable. Independently recomputed; matches the 0.93 recorded in
evidence_plan.md.

## Adjudication (Claude Vision, worst-15 score-1 divergences)

Verdicts: [`adjudication.md`](ocrq_full/../ocrq_full/adjudication.md). Summary: ~half the
worst divergences are the 72B reference's fault (duplication, repetition loops, chat
preamble, LaTeX-vs-plain format). Two genuine failure modes of ours, both Vision-confirmed:
blank-page formula hallucination (idx 924/967/969) and an `[illegible]` repetition loop
(idx 654). Both were quarantined from the gated/searchable product by the ink-gate
(verified: 0 gated segments on 924) and both are now guarded at source (PR #21) — the
guards' end-to-end in-situ verification is recorded below.

## In-situ guard verification (2026-07-06, post-#21 code) — PASSED

The four real failure pages re-run through the FULL pipeline (fresh out_dir, no resume
cache, live MLX server), closing the evidence_plan "unit-tested, not yet verified through
the full stage stack" item:

| idx | det segments | VLM called? | vlm_reading | document.md |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 924 | 0 | **no** (short-circuit, 0.0s) | empty | no formula; 25 chars (page furniture only) |
| 967 | 0 | **no** (0.0s) | empty | no formula |
| 969 | 0 | **no** (0.0s) | empty | no formula |
| 654 | 4 | yes (93.4s) | **empty — degenerate read discarded** | no loop; det_text fallback (67 chars) |

Both guards behave as designed through the full stack: blank pages never reach the VLM
(hallucination class removed at source + a saved call each); the 654 repetition loop
recurred but was discarded by the degenerate-read guard, so fusion fell back to det_text
and nothing degenerate reached `document.md`.

**Perf note:** the repetition guard is post-hoc — 654 still burned 93s of generation to
the token cap before the output was discarded. Correctness is done; if loop pages turn out
to be common, the refinement is a lower `max_tokens` for retried/escalated reads or a
streaming abort. Not urgent at current volume.

## Claim hygiene

Valid: harness-bug discovery (CJK tokenisation), failure-mode discovery (blank-page /
repetition hallucination), ink-gate P2 confirmation on real instances, reference-quality
characterisation, hand-label targeting. **Not valid:** any absolute accuracy claim — the
reference is a VLM's output. Accuracy claims route through FUNSD/SROIE/hand labels
(evidence_plan streams A–C).
